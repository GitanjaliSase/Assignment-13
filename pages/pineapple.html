Pineapple is native to tropical and subtropical America and has been introduced elsewhere.
The fruit has become a characteristic ingredient in the meat, vegetable, fish, and rice dishes of what is loosely termed Pan-Asian cuisine. The fruit is eaten fresh where available and in canned form worldwide.
ineapples grow in tropical (warm) areas around the world. The leading producers of pineapples include Thailand, the Philippines, India, China, Brazil, and the U.S. state of Hawaii. Pineapples grow on plants that have leaves shaped like long swords. The plants also have purple flowers and smaller leaves.
